Banana Bread

Description

This is a great high protien version of banana bread that can be eaten at any time throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 serving nonstick cooking spray
  • 1 1/4 cup oat flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 scoops of chocolate protein powder
  • 3 Teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 overripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 3 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray an 9x5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray
  2. Mix together oat flour, sugar, walnuts, protein powder, cinnamon, baking powder, and baking soad in a medium bowl
  3. Mix togetehr mashed bananas, applesauce, milk, egg whites, and vanilla extract in a large bowl. Slowly add flour mixture; stirring until just combined. Transfer batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until top of bread springs back when lightly pressed and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes. Cool in pan for 10 minutes. Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
